# Quarterly Performance Review

## Executive Summary

This report covers Q1 2026 operational performance across all business units. Revenue targets were met in two of three divisions, with the Services division exceeding forecast by 12%.

Key highlights:

- **Total revenue:** $4.2M (target: $4.0M)
- **Customer satisfaction:** 92% (up from 87%)
- **Employee retention:** 96%

## Revenue by Division

| Division | Target | Actual | Variance |
|----------|--------|--------|----------|
| Products | $1.8M | $1.7M | -5.6% |
| Services | $1.2M | $1.34M | +11.7% |
| Consulting | $1.0M | $1.16M | +16.0% |
| **Total** | **$4.0M** | **$4.2M** | **+5.0%** |

## Key Metrics

### Customer Satisfaction

Customer NPS improved from 42 to 51 over the quarter, driven primarily by improvements in response time and first-call resolution rates.

> "The new support workflow has cut our average resolution time from 4.2 hours to 1.8 hours. Customers are noticing." — Head of Support

### Employee Engagement

The quarterly pulse survey showed engagement scores steady at 4.1/5.0 across all teams. Notable improvements in the Engineering team (+0.3) offset a slight decline in Sales (-0.1).

## Risks and Concerns

1. **Supply chain delays** — Component lead times have increased from 6 to 10 weeks. Mitigation: increased safety stock for critical parts.
2. **Competitive pressure** — Two new entrants in the mid-market segment. Response: accelerated feature roadmap for Q2.
3. **Talent pipeline** — Three senior engineering roles unfilled for 60+ days. Action: expanded recruiting partnerships.

## Q2 Outlook

Forecast for Q2 remains positive:

- Revenue target: $4.5M (+7% QoQ)
- Three new product launches planned
- Office expansion in Melbourne complete by June

### Strategic Priorities

1. Launch Product Alpha v2.0
2. Close partnership with TechCo
3. Complete SOC 2 Type II certification
4. Hire remaining engineering positions

## Appendix

### Methodology

All financial figures are unaudited and based on internal accounting systems. Customer satisfaction scores are derived from post-interaction surveys (n=1,247 responses this quarter).

### Glossary

- **NPS** — Net Promoter Score, measures customer loyalty on a -100 to +100 scale
- **QoQ** — Quarter over Quarter comparison
- **Pulse survey** — Short employee engagement survey conducted quarterly
